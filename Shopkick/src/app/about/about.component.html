How it works

Want to earn Shopkick points by not walking to the stores to scan products? Someone has just made your job easier by scanning and uploading barcodes for products across all your favorite shopping destinations. All you have to do is select your store and the required product category and start using the barcodes to scan and upload to shopkick to earn shopkick points.
